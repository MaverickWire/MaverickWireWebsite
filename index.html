Design

At Maverick Wire, we focus on the presentation of our product. That's why every roll of Purple Gard® barbed wire has a high quality plastic reel, a reflective purple plastic coating that adds glamour to the product, and a label that tells shoppers everything they need to know about our product.

Quality

We use only the best wire for our products. Our Purple Gard® barbed wire uses Class 1 galvanized wire that is coated with a UV protected purple polymer alloy, which prolongs the useful life of the barbed wire, and prevents rust and color fading.

Made in the USA

Our barbed wire rolls are produced at our Missouri facility from components manufactured in the USA, so you can be sure that you are supporting companies located in the United States.
